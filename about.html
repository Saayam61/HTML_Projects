Our Story


Founded in 2005, New Leaf Grocery embarked on a remarkable journey with a simple yet powerful vision – to provide families with access to fresh, high-quality groceries that nourish both body and soul. It all began in a quaint corner of our local community, where a small market was born from a shared passion for supporting local farmers and producers.

Every step of our journey has been driven by a deep commitment to offering more than just groceries – we are dedicated to providing a connection to the land, the people, and the stories behind the food we offer. From the very beginning, we recognized the importance of bridging the gap between local farmers and the families who appreciate their hard work.

As we grew, so did our family of customers, farmers, and partners. The values that fueled our inception remain at the heart of everything we do: quality, community, sustainability, and customer well-being. With each passing year, our roots have grown deeper, intertwining with the rich tapestry of the neighborhoods we serve.

From those early days to today, New Leaf Grocery continues to thrive as a testament to the dedication and passion of our team, our loyal customers, and the local farmers who entrust us with their bountiful harvests. We invite you to join us on this journey of taste, discovery, and connection as we redefine the way you experience grocery shopping.

Welcome to New Leaf Grocery – where every product tells a story and every customer becomes part of our narrative. Thank you for being a vital chapter in our ongoing story of growth, community, and fresh possibilities.

Quality & Sustainability


At New Leaf Grocery, we believe that quality is not just a standard – it's a promise we deliver with unwavering commitment. Our dedication to providing you with the best doesn't stop at offering a wide array of fresh and flavorful products; it extends to the very essence of how we curate our offerings.

From the vibrant colors of hand-picked produce to the rich textures of artisanal goods, every item on our shelves is a testament to our pursuit of excellence. We source our products from trusted local suppliers who share our values of sustainability and ethical practices. We believe that the journey from farm to table is a sacred one, and it's our responsibility to ensure that you receive nothing but the best.

Our partnerships with local farmers and producers are rooted in the principles of fairness, transparency, and mutual growth. By supporting these local businesses, we not only provide you with the freshest options but also contribute to the well-being of the community we serve.

When you choose New Leaf Grocery, you're not just choosing superior quality – you're making an eco-conscious choice that echoes our commitment to the planet. From reducing packaging waste to promoting reusable and eco-friendly alternatives, we're dedicated to minimizing our ecological footprint.

Join us in embracing a future where quality and sustainability are not just choices, but a way of life. As we strive to redefine your grocery experience, rest assured that every purchase you make contributes to a more flavorful, responsible, and harmonious world.

At New Leaf Grocery, your journey to better living is nurtured by the harmony between nature's bounty and our dedication to preserving it for generations to come.

Community & Outreach


At New Leaf Grocery, our roots run deep within the community that has embraced us since day one. We believe that a successful business is not solely defined by profits; it's also measured by the positive impact it leaves on the lives of those it serves. That's why community engagement and outreach have been integral parts of our journey.

We're proud to be more than just a grocery store – we're a catalyst for change. Our commitment to giving back to the community is evident in our active participation in various charitable initiatives. We believe that no one should go to bed hungry, which is why we regularly contribute surplus food to local charities and organize food drives to support those in need.

But our efforts don't stop there. We collaborate with community organizations, schools, and local events to foster a sense of togetherness and mutual support. Our partnerships extend beyond the store's walls, as we believe in building a network that thrives on shared values and aspirations.

Whether it's supporting local schools' nutrition programs or actively participating in neighborhood events, we're dedicated to making a meaningful difference. We view our customers as more than just patrons – they're our neighbors, friends, and partners in creating a stronger, healthier, and more vibrant community.

As you shop with us, you're not only nourishing your body but also contributing to the well-being of your neighbors. At New Leaf Grocery, community is at the heart of everything we do. Join us in making our community a better place, one purchase at a time.
